Om Ah Ra Pa Tsa Na Dhih: Manjushri is the bodhisattva of Wisdom. 
The Mantra helps enhance various wisdoms. 
It helps clear the mind cutting through delusion to discover 
the true nature of reality or the true nature of the self. 
That true nature being emptiness. This teaching is fundamental to all forms of Buddhism 
and is often called “Mother of All Buddhas”
