Vajrasattva – Or the One Hundred Syllable Manta is known as the Prince of Purity. 
He represents the perfect union of Wisdom and Compassion. 
Repeating  this mantra as many as 100,000 times (or more) is a powerful tool to purify karma.
It can pave the way to the perfect understanding of the true nature of reality (emptiness) 
and bodhichitta (ultimate compassion).
